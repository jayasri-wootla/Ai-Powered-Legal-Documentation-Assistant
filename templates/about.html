About LegalDoc AI

LegalDoc AI is an AI-powered legal documentation assistant designed to help users analyze contracts, detect risks, identify important clauses, and generate professional legal documents.

🎯 Project Objective

To simplify legal contract analysis using Artificial Intelligence and provide automated insights including summaries, risks, and improvement suggestions.

⚙️ Technologies Used

Flask, Python, Groq AI, SQLite, ReportLab, and HTML/CSS are used to build this intelligent legal assistant system.

🚀 Key Features

Contract Summary, Clause Detection, Risk Analysis, AI Chat, Legal Document Generation, and Improvement Suggestions.
